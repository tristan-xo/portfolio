PELTIER COOLER FOR GAMING LAPTOPS

Developed a smart cooling system using a Peltier module to regulate temperature efficiently. Initially used a DHT11 sensor but upgraded to read GPU temperature directly. The module activates at 55°C and deactivates at 35°C, with a fan running 5 minutes post-shutdown. Integrated a 16x2 I2C LCD for temperature display and system status. Currently adding IoT capabilities via ESP8266 and MQTT for remote monitoring, showcasing skills in automation, embedded systems, and IoT.

3D PRINTED VERNIER CALIPER

Designed and developed a cost-effective vernier caliper by simplifying its structure and optimizing production costs while maintaining high measurement accuracy. Utilized Fusion 360 for 3D modeling and design refinements, followed by prototype testing to ensure precision. The project highlights expertise in mechanical design, precision engineering, and fabrication techniques. The remodeled vernier caliper received positive feedback for its practicality and efficiency, demonstrating its potential for real-world applications.
